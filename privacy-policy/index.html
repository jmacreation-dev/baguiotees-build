PRIVACY
POLICY

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or interact with us through Facebook Messenger.

Information We Collect

  • Cookies and Website Data: When you visit our website, we use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze website traffic, and remember your preferences. This includes browsing data and website usage information.
  • Facebook Messenger Information: When you communicate with us through Facebook Chat Messenger, we may collect and store the following information that you provide to us:
    • Full name
    • Contact information (phone number, email address)
    • Shipping address

How We Use Your Information

  • Website Cookies: We use cookies to improve website functionality, analyze visitor behavior, and provide a personalized browsing experience.
  • Order Processing: Information collected through Facebook Messenger (name, contact, and address) is used solely to process and fulfill your orders, including shipping and customer service communications.
  • Customer Service: We use your contact information to communicate with you about your orders, respond to inquiries, and provide customer support.

Purchase Information

  • Facebook Platform: All purchases are made directly through the Facebook platform. We do not process payments on our website.
  • No Purchase Data Storage: We do not save purchase transaction data, payment information, or order details in our database. All purchase-related data is handled by Facebook's platform in accordance with Facebook's privacy policies.
  • Shipping Information Only: The only information we store from Facebook Messenger conversations is your full name, contact information, and shipping address, which is necessary to fulfill your orders.

Data Storage and Security

  • We only store the information you provide through Facebook Messenger (name, contact, and address) that is necessary for order fulfillment.
  • We implement appropriate security measures to protect the limited personal information we store.
  • We do not store payment information, credit card details, or purchase transaction data.
  • All purchase transactions are processed securely through Facebook's platform.

Cookies and Tracking

  • We use cookies on our website to enhance your browsing experience, remember your preferences, and analyze website traffic patterns.
  • You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent.
  • Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of certain website features.
  • We do not use cookies to track your activity on other websites or for advertising purposes beyond our own website analytics.

Third-Party Services

  • Facebook Platform: When you make a purchase through Facebook, your transaction data is handled by Facebook in accordance with their privacy policy. We do not have access to or store your payment information.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.
  • We may use third-party services for website analytics, but these services only collect anonymous, aggregated data about website usage.

Your Rights

  • You have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you (name, contact, and address).
  • You can request that we update or correct any inaccurate information.
  • You can request that we delete your personal information from our records, subject to any legal obligations we may have to retain certain information.
  • You can opt out of marketing communications at any time by contacting us through Facebook Messenger.

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, how we handle your data, or wish to exercise your rights regarding your personal information, please contact us through Facebook Messenger. We are committed to addressing your privacy concerns promptly and transparently.

Policy Updates

  • We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements.
  • Significant changes will be communicated through our website or Facebook page.
  • The "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy indicates when it was last revised.
  • Continued use of our website or services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
